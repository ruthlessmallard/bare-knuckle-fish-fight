# Bare Knuckle Fish Fight

A ghost possesses office workers to save their goldfish. Mid-century office chaos.

## Quick Start

```bash
flutter pub get
flutter run
```

Or grab the latest APK from GitHub Actions.

## How to Play

**Mobile (Thumb Controls):**
- Touch and drag to aim
- Lift your thumb, then tap again in the same spot to fire
- Your possessed body follows your aim

**Desktop (Keyboard/Mouse):**
- WASD or Arrow keys to move
- Mouse to aim
- Click to shoot

## The Game

You're dead. They have your goldfish. You can't touch anything... but you CAN possess people.

- Possess enemies to gain their weapons
- When your host dies, jump to a new one
- Enemies will shoot their possessed friends
- Navigate the mid-century office and rescue your fish
